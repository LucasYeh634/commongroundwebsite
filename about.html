About

Built from years of listening.

The idea behind CommonGround grew out of music, service, medicine, and conversations across generations.

Music became a way into community.

I grew up surrounded by music and eventually performed in concert halls, homes, hospitals, community gatherings, and senior residences. The settings changed, but the pattern remained: people connected most deeply when they felt invited to participate, speak, and be heard.

CommonGround is my attempt to carry that lesson into technology.

“Music wasn't creating connection; it was giving people a reason to connect.”
CommonGround

An early concept exploring how technology can strengthen meaningful community.
