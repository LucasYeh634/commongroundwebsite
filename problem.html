The problem

Connected, but not always close

Attention over authenticity

Many platforms reward clicks, constant posting, and endless scrolling rather than trust and meaningful conversation.

Large networks, weak ties

It is possible to follow thousands of people while still feeling unseen, unheard, or disconnected.

Generational distance

People of different ages often have valuable stories to share but few natural places to meet and listen.

Online without offline

Digital interaction should help people participate in their neighborhoods, schools, hospitals, and communities.
